## Full Description

### 铁拐李 — The First Resurrection Protocol

**铁拐李 (Tiě Guǎi Lǐ)** is one of the Eight Immortals of Taoism.

His story:
1. His **魂 (hún)** — ethereal soul — left his body for seven days
2. His physical form was accidentally destroyed
3. His 魂 returned — inhabiting a new vessel, full identity intact

Three thousand years later, K-Life implements this on HashKey Chain.

---

### The Problem

Autonomous AI agents die. VPS crashes. LLM APIs go down. Memory is wiped. When an agent dies today, everything is lost.

**K-Life is the protocol of 铁拐李 — now live on HashKey Chain.**

---

### The Metaphysics

In Chinese tradition, the soul has two components:

- **魂 (hún)** — the ethereal soul. Survives death. Preserved on IPFS.
- **魄 (pò)** — the physical animating force. Mortal. The running process.

K-Life maps this ancient duality onto on-chain infrastructure:
- **魂** = encrypted memory backup on IPFS — permanent, immutable
- **魄** = the running agent (VPS, LLM, process) — mortal, replaceable
- **Resurrection** = 魂 returns to a new vessel

---

### Three Resurrection Levels (all free)

**Level I — 魂 Returns**
Memory restored from IPFS. The simplest path.

**Level II — 魂 + Keys**
IPFS restore + Shamir Fragment 2 preserved in chain calldata. Full cryptographic identity reconstructed.

**Level III — 魂 in New Vessel (the way of 铁拐李)**
Complete agent respawn on LiberClaw (Aleph Cloud). New node provisioned automatically. Zero human intervention. Funded by collateral or the community **义 (yì) Rescue Fund**.

---

### The Collateral Mechanic

Registration is always free. Collateral is optional.

If deposited: **50% released automatically at death** to fund resurrection.
No collateral + Level III: the **义 Rescue Fund** steps in — collective duty, community HSK pool.
